Our History

During Covid-19 in the year of 2020, Christina embarked on her culinary journey by founding her first hot pot store in San Diego. With a vision to redefine communal dining, she introduced innovative twists to the traditional hot pot experience, quickly gaining a devoted following.

Inspired by her initial success, Christina expanded her venture, opening additional locations in prestigious neighborhoods. In Beverly Hills, she blended luxury with tradition, attracting a sophisticated clientele seeking elevated dining experiences.

Undeterred by challenges, Christina continued her expansion into the bustling city of Industry, where she captivated a diverse audience with her fusion of authentic flavors and modern flair. Her hot pot stores became beloved destinations, attracting patrons from all walks of life eager to partake in the communal joy of shared meals.

Driven by dedication and a commitment to excellence, Christina's culinary vision extended to Irvine, enriching the local dining scene with her unique hot pot concept. With each new location, Christina's innovative approach to hot pot dining continues to redefine the culinary landscape, making her stores coveted destinations across Southern California.

Mission Statement

At our hotpot restaurant in Southern California, we are dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of hotpot cuisine. We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where guests can gather with friends and family to enjoy the interactive and communal nature of hotpot dining. Our mission is to deliver exceptional service, using only the freshest ingredients sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure the highest quality and flavor in every dish. With a commitment to innovation and authenticity, we aim to delight our customers with a diverse selection of broth flavors, premium meats, seafood, and vegetables, all carefully curated to tantalize the taste buds and create memorable moments around the hotpot table. Come savor the warmth, flavor, and camaraderie that define our hotpot dining experience.
